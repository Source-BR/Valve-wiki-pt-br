<span style="font-weight:bold; color:white">Hammer</span> (version <span style="font-weight:bold; color:white">5.x</span>), sometimes informally called '''Hammer 2''', is [[Valve]]'s in-house level editor for {{source2|1.bold}} games. Compared to its predecessor, '''[[Hammer|Hammer 4.x]]''' (on [[Source]]), the user interface has been redesigned using {{w|icon=0|Qt}} and the workflow can differ a lot from the old, [[BSP]]-oriented process. In many ways, it is closer to that of modern modelling suites commonly used in the industry. 

'''Hammer 5.x''' is available in Workshop Tools DLC for games that support it, namely {{hla|1.bold}}, {{cs2|1.bold}}, {{dota2|1.bold}}, etc.
